ABOUT ME

As a teenager, a friend and mentor introduced me to fingerstyle guitar. I had been playing the guitar for a while, but this was the key that unlocked my creativity. The experience taught me the lasting impact someone could have through teaching music and as a music instructor, this is what inspires me. I want to share my knowledge and love of music with others, while helping them to discover its many joys. I offer guitar, banjo and voice instruction out of our family home in Columbia, Missouri.
